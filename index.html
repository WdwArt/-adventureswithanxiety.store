About The Game

Adventures With Anxiety is an interactive story created by Nicky Case that offers a unique perspective on mental health. In this game, you don't play as the person experiencing anxiety - you play AS the anxiety itself!

Through engaging storytelling and meaningful choices, the game helps players understand the complex nature of anxiety and how it affects our daily lives and relationships.

Key Features

Unique Perspective: Experience anxiety from a completely different angle by playing as the anxiety itself.

Engaging Narrative: Enjoy a well-crafted story that balances humor with insightful commentary on mental health.

Meaningful Choices: Your decisions as the anxiety character influence the story and outcomes.

How To Play

Simply click the embedded game above or use the "Play Full Screen" button for a better experience. The game is browser-based and requires no downloads or installations. Follow the on-screen instructions to navigate through the story and make choices.

Tip: For the best experience, play in a quiet environment where you can focus on the story and emotional journey.

About The Creator

Adventures With Anxiety was created by Nicky Case, a developer known for making interactive experiences that explore complex topics in accessible ways. This game is free to play and was made with the goal of helping people better understand anxiety and mental health.

"I made this game to help folks understand their anxiety, and maybe even learn to live healthily with it."

- Nicky Case
